Ava Augustin

Ava Augustin is an award winning Canadian actress who has worked alongside many of Hollywood and Canada’s top actors such as, Tatiana Maslany, Javicia Leslie, and Chantel Riley.

Ava has been involved in the industry from as early as six months of age with her work ranging from print to acting in film, television and commercials.

About

Ava's most recent projects include the role of 'Scout' in the hit Netflix series Scaredy Cats and ‘Young Ryan’ in CW’s Batwoman television series.

Ava also voices the character of Tickety Tock on the live action/ animated children series Blue’s Clues & You.

A few of her accomplishments in acting include winning best actress at both the Hollywood Young Entertainer Awards and the Vancouver Joey Awards as well as walking the red carpet for her starring role in the short film “A Rock and a Hard Place”.

Ava is passionate about acting and is an ambassador for TBFF Kids Film Festival. She draws inspiration from her big sister and fellow actress Allison Augustin.

Ava loves spending her free time making Tik Toks with friends and family, and also competing in Provincial level gymnastics.
